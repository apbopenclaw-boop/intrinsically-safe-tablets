Quick Verdict

Choose Tab-Ex 03 DZ2 if:

  • • You need a 10.1″ screen for drawings, forms, or SCADA
  • • Your organisation uses Samsung Knox or DeX
  • • You want the Samsung Galaxy Tab ecosystem
  • • Maximum global support network matters

Choose IS930.1 if:

  • • You need a compact, one-hand-holdable tablet
  • • Weight matters for all-day carry (570g vs 690g)

Screen Size: The Defining Difference

The Tab-Ex 03 DZ2's 10.1-inch display is its biggest advantage. For field engineers reviewing P&ID drawings, filling inspection checklists, or running SCADA dashboards, the extra screen real estate makes a measurable difference in efficiency. The ecom also supports Samsung DeX, allowing desktop-mode operation when docked.

The IS930.1's 8-inch display is a deliberate design choice — it's compact enough to hold in one hand while climbing ladders, working in confined spaces, or walking between process units. If your field workers primarily need a camera, barcode scanner, and simple data entry, the smaller form factor is an advantage, not a limitation.

Enterprise Management

The Tab-Ex 03 DZ2 is built on a Samsung Galaxy Tab Active, which means it inherits Samsung Knox — hardware-backed security, remote lock/wipe, and tight integration with Samsung's enterprise management tools. For organisations already running Samsung devices across offices and field sites, the Tab-Ex fits into the existing fleet.

The IS930.1 uses standard Android Enterprise, compatible with Microsoft Intune, VMware Workspace ONE, and other MDM platforms. It's more vendor-neutral but lacks Knox's hardware root of trust.

Price and Total Cost of Ownership

At approximately €2,400 vs €3,200, the IS930.1 is roughly 25% less expensive per unit. For a fleet of 50 tablets, that's a €40,000 difference in upfront cost alone. However, TCO depends on more than purchase price — consider accessory ecosystems, case replacement costs, and Samsung's typically longer software update commitment.

For a full pricing breakdown across all ATEX tablets, see our ATEX tablet pricing comparison.

Bottom Line

The Tab-Ex 03 DZ2 is the better choice for inspection workflows, SCADA access, and Samsung-centric enterprises. The larger screen justifies the price premium when field workers spend hours reviewing documents and forms.

The IS930.1 is the better choice for mobile-first field workers who need a compact, lightweight device they can carry all day — and where budget matters. It's also the better fit for organisations that want a purpose-built Ex device rather than a modified consumer tablet.
